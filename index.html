Priorities
  • Candidate Recruitment - We have already begun a detailed plan to recruit and train candidates for 2025 municipal elections. We need to carry the momentum to recruit for 2027.
  • College Democrats- The number of College Democrat chapters has doubled in 2023. We need to continue to support and connect with chapters across the state.
  • Voter Education- We’ve expanded our methods of reaching voters to include digital tools such as QR codes, along with more traditional methods such as push cards. We need to continue expanding to reach GEN Z and Millennial voters.
  • Voter Engagement Committee-As a recently appointed member of the Democratic Party’s Voter engagement committee, I have the opportunity to work with Democrats across the country examining various states’ legislation on voter issues
